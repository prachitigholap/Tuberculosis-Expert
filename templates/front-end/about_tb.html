ABOUT TUBERCULOSIS


Tuberculosis (TB) is a contagious infection that usually attacks your lungs. It can also spread to other parts of your body, like your brain and spine. A type of bacteria called Mycobacterium tuberculosis causes it.


SYMPTOMS OF TUBERCULOSIS


Signs of Active TB are :

  • A cough that lasts more than 3 weeks
  • Chest pain
  • Coughing up blood
  • Constant Fever
  • Loss of appetite
  • Feeling tired all the time
  • Weight loss
  • Night sweats
  • Chills


    • TREATMENT OF TUBERCULOSIS


    ~ For Latent TB - Recommended treatment with with medication if you're at high risk of developing a Active TB.
    ~ For Active TB - Must take prescribed antibiotics for at least six to nine months.
