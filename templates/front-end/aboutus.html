ABOUT US


This is website helps a person predict whether they have tuberculosis or not based on their chest X-ray. This web app is used for diagnosis of Pulmonary Tuberculosis using Image Processing and Deep Learning. The detection happens in two phases. In the first phase, the Xray Image is preprocessed with Binary & Otsu Thresholding with Histogram Equalization. The second phase consists of the prediction of tuberculosis on the pretrained model.
